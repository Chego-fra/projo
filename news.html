Your guide to the month ahead on PS4 and PS5, including new releases,in-depth features and expert guides.

ASTRO BOT

Join ASTRO in a brand-new, supersized space adventure as you ride your trusty Dual Speeder across more than 50 planets full of fun, danger and surprises. On your journey, make the most of ASTRO’s new powers and reunite with many iconic heroes from the PlayStation universe!

The Casting of Frank Stone

The storytelling prowess of Supermassive Games meets the Dead by Daylight universe as a group of young friends discover Frank Stone’s blood-soaked legacy. Explore the town of Cedar Hills and uncover the generational scars left across a whole community irrevocably altered by violence.

NBA 2K25

NBA 2K25’s ProPLAY technology means your favorite NBA players’ signature moves look more realistic than ever and players can fine-tune their skills to deliver career-defining plays. Immerse yourself in the most authentic NBA experience to date.

Dead Rising Deluxe Remaster

More than just a remaster, this Deluxe Remaster is a full graphical overhaul of the first game in zombie-slaughtering action game series Dead Rising. This remaster has also been fully voiced, supports auto-saves, and has various other quality-of-life features.

Join voice chat on your PS5

Jump straight into Discord voice chat on your PS5 console. Join directly, now with no mobile or PC app required to initiate the connection.

Head to the PS5 Control Center, select the Discord tab in Game Base, and link your account for PlayStation Network to Discord to get started.
